Vegetable Manchurian Recipe

  • Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup finely chopped vegetables (carrot, cabbage, bell pepper, beans)
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • For the sauce: 2 tablespoons soy sauce, 1 tablespoon chili sauce, 1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chopped vegetables with all-purpose flour, cornflour, soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well to form a batter.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Drop spoonfuls of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. For the sauce, combine soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, and cornstarch in a bowl. Heat a small amount of oil in a pan, add the sauce mixture, and cook until thickened.
  4. Toss the fried Manchurian balls in the sauce. Serve hot.
