Honey Chilli Potatoes Recipe

  • Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the potato wedges with all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the potato wedges until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  3. In a separate pan, heat a small amount of oil and add ginger, garlic, and green chilies (if using). Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add soy sauce, honey, chili sauce, vinegar, and salt. Stir well and cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Toss the fried potatoes in the sauce until well coated. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
