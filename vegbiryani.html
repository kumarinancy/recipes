Veg Biryani Recipe

  • Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potatoes)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat ghee or oil. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  3. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Stir in the biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they start to soften.
  7. Add the yogurt and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil. Add the soaked rice and cook until it’s 70% cooked. Drain the rice.
  9. Layer the partially cooked rice over the vegetable mixture in the pot. Sprinkle chopped coriander and mint leaves on top.
  10. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  11. Turn off the heat and let the biryani sit covered for 5 minutes before serving.
