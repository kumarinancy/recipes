Vegetable Noodles Recipe

  • Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, cabbage, beans)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 spring onions, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium heat. Add ginger and garlic, and stir-fry for a minute.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the pan and toss everything together until well mixed and heated through.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions if desired. Serve hot.
