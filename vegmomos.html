Kurkure Momos Recipe

  • Back to Home

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • For the Filling:
  • 1 cup finely chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrots, bell peppers)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. For the dough, mix flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead to form a smooth dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. For the filling, heat oil in a pan and saut√© ginger-garlic paste for a minute. Add vegetables and cook until tender. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well and remove from heat.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls. Roll each ball into a thin circle. Place a spoonful of filling in the center, fold and seal the edges
